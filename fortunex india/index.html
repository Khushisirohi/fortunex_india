Our Services

Fortunex India offers end-to-end export services, ensuring seamless sourcing, manufacturing, and distribution of high-quality apparel and textiles. With state-of-the-art manufacturing units and a skilled workforce, the company guarantees precision, durability, and efficiency in production. Its services include quality control, custom manufacturing, and compliance with international standards, ensuring that clients receive products tailored to their specific requirements. Backed by a strong supply chain and advanced logistics, Fortunex India provides efficient warehousing, packaging, and global shipping solutions. The company prioritizes timely deliveries and streamlined export processes, making it a reliable partner for fashion brands, retail chains, and wholesalers worldwide. With a commitment to ethical and sustainable practices, Fortunex India continues to uphold excellence in the global apparel export industry.

Sustainability Commitment

Fortunex India is committed to sustainability by integrating eco-friendly practices into its manufacturing and export processes. The company prioritizes the use of organic fabrics, recycled materials, and energy-efficient production methods to minimize environmental impact. By working with ethical suppliers and reducing waste, Fortunex India ensures responsible sourcing and promotes sustainable fashion on a global scale. With a focus on innovation, Fortunex India offers customized sustainable solutions for brands looking to reduce their carbon footprint. From eco-conscious packaging to fair trade production, the company provides high-quality, ethically produced apparel that meets international sustainability standards while maintaining style and durability.

About Us

Founded in 2010 by industry veteran Rachna Anand, Fortunex India is a trusted export house specializing in women’s and kids’ apparel, men’s wear, and fashion accessories. With 26 years of experience, Rachna has built Fortunex into a name known for quality, craftsmanship, and reliability. Operating multiple manufacturing units that focus on knits and traditional Indian garment arts, Fortunex India has proudly served top brands across North America and Europe. Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction continues to drive us as we expand globally
